The Surfer

This project was part of a class called server-side web applications. I was part of a group of three who chose to work on our own custom project. We had an idea for a surfer site that will provide surfers with news and weather information based on their location and areas of interest. The web pages were developed using html, css, javascript and ajax. It is also connected to a mySql database. We used a model-view-controller framework. I contributed in all the different parts of the project from styling to database construction.

Date: fall 2011
